Japan

Japan is an island in East Asia.Located in the Pacific Ocean, Japan is the world's 4th largest island country and encompasses about 6,852 islands.Japan is the 2nd most populous island country. The population of approximately 126 million is the world's eleventh largest, of which 98.5% are ethnic Japanese. 90.7% of people live in cities, while 9.3% live in the countryside.The 3 of the most popular city is Tokyo, Fukouka and Niigata.Tokyo is the most busy capital, mixes the ultramodern and the traditional, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples.The Area is 2,188 km² and Population: 9.273 million (2015).Fukuoka It’s known for ancient temples, beaches and modern shopping malls, including Canal City. Maizuru Park contains ruins of 17th-century Fukuoka Castle.The area is 4,987 km² and Population: 5.109 million (Jun 1, 2019). Niigata is Japan’s main island. The Minatopia history museum complex includes heritage buildings like the 19th-century Niigata Customs House. Along the Sea of Japan coast, the Marinepia Nihonkai aquarium has sea otters and dolphins.The area is 726.4 km² and Population: 810,157 (2015) .
